# Strategy 6 — Selling Knowledge (Courses, Prompts, Systems)

## Productize What You Know

Client work trades time for money. Knowledge products trade value for freedom.

Every project teaches you something. Every problem you solve becomes a system. Every breakthrough you make can help someone else break through.

The question isn't "What do I know?" It's "What do I know that others need?"

## When to Start

Most wait too long. Here's the truth:

**You're ready when:**

- People ask "How did you do that?" regularly.
- You solve the same problems repeatedly.
- Your DMs fill with process questions.
- You've proven results publicly.
- You catch yourself teaching for free.

You don't need to be the world expert. Just two steps ahead of your audience.

## What Actually Sells

The market doesn't care about your knowledge. It cares about its problems.

### High-Value Prompt Systems

The instant gratification play.

- **What works:** Style-specific prompt packs, industry-focused templates, workflow accelerators, consistency frameworks.
- **Price range:** $47-297
- **Why they buy:** Immediate results, no learning curve.

### Short Creative Workflows

The transformation shortcut.

- **What works:** 30-minute process videos, step-by-step breakdowns, tool combination guides, speed techniques.
- **Price range:** $97-497
- **Why they buy:** A clear path from A to B.

### System Blueprints

The business builder.

- **What works:** Complete creative systems, client management templates, pricing calculators, project frameworks.
- **Price range:** $297-997
- **Why they buy:** Proven systems that scale.

### Mini-Courses + Templates

The deep dive.

- **What works:** Focused skill building, actionable frameworks, real project walkthroughs, community access.
- **Price range:** $497-2,997
- **Why they buy:** Transformation, not just information.

## The Value Equation

Price isn't about pages. It's about problems solved.

**Value multipliers:**

- Time saved (quantify it)
- Money earned (prove it)
- Mistakes avoided (list them)
- Results accelerated (show them)

A 10-page system that saves 20 hours monthly is worth more than a 200-page theory book.

## Your Proven Model

Real products that work:

- **The Prompt Language Engine:** A specific system for consistent results with a clear value proposition and immediate implementation.
- **AI Visual Mastery:** A comprehensive transformation with multiple learning paths, a community component, and ongoing value delivery.

Both solve real problems. Both deliver measurable results. Both sell because they work.

## Packaging for Profit

How you present determines what people pay.

- **Basic package:** Just the content (PDF or video, DIY implementation, email support).
- **Premium package:** Content + context (main training, templates, case studies, group calls).
- **VIP package:** Content + transformation (everything above, plus 1-on-1 sessions and custom workflows).

Same core content. 3x-5x price difference.

## The Launch Sequence

Launching isn't an event. It's a campaign.

- **Week -4:** Seed the idea. Share wins from your system and build anticipation.
- **Week -2:** Open cart. Present a clear offer, share social proof, and use limited time/quantity.
- **Week 0:** Close strong. Send final value reminders, increase urgency, and reveal bonuses.
- **Week +2:** Deliver excellence. Exceed expectations, capture testimonials, and plan the next iteration.

Each launch teaches you what to improve.

## Recurring Revenue Plays

One-time sales are good. Recurring revenue is better.

**Models that work:**

- **Prompt Library Membership:** New prompts monthly, style updates, technique videos, community access. Price: $29-97/month.
- **Creative Community:** Weekly workshops, peer feedback, resource sharing, direct access to you. Price: $97-297/month.
- **Implementation Program:** Cohort-based learning with accountability and live sessions. Price: $497-997/month.

Stack recurring revenue. Sleep better.

## The Trust Requirement

Knowledge products live or die on trust.

**Trust builders:**

- Free value consistently delivered
- Results shown, not claimed
- Student success highlighted
- Refund policy prominent
- Under-promise, over-deliver

**Trust killers:**

- Hype without substance
- Hidden costs
- Fake scarcity
- No student results
- Over-promise, under-deliver

Your reputation is worth more than any single sale.

## Scaling Your Expertise

Success creates opportunities:

- Level 1: Sell what you know.
- Level 2: Sell what works.
- Level 3: Sell transformation.
- Level 4: Sell certification.
- Level 5: Sell the system to sell.

Each level multiplies value and the price point.

## Common Knowledge Product Mistakes

- **Mistake 1:** Making it too broad.
  - **Fix:** Solve one problem deeply.
- **Mistake 2:** All theory, no action.
  - **Fix:** Include templates, examples, and exercises.
- **Mistake 3:** Underpricing value.
  - **Fix:** Price for transformation, not time.
- **Mistake 4:** Launch and abandon.
  - **Fix:** Iterate based on feedback.
- **Mistake 5:** Competing on price.
  - **Fix:** Compete on results.
