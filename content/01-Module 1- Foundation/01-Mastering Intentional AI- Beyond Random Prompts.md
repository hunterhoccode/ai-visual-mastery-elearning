# Mastering Intentional AI: Beyond Random Prompts

## The Problem with AI Images

Let's cut to the chase: 95% of AI images you see online are garbage. They're technically functional but creatively bankrupt. No soul, no intention, no system. Just random prompts thrown at a machine, hoping for magic.

This isn't about the tools being broken. The tools are fine. It's about the humans using them like slot machines instead of precision instruments.

## Taking Creative Control

This module isn't here to teach you how to generate "cool" images. Anyone can do that. We're here to teach you how to take creative control. How to stop getting lucky and start getting consistent. How to build visual systems that scale.

Here's what separates the pros from the prompt-spammers: intention.
